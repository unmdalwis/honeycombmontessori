ABOUT US

At Honeycomb Montessori, we believe your child is special. With their unique personality and high level of intelligence, we encourage our students academically, physically, socially, and mentally.

We also pride ourselves on our application of Dr. Maria Montessori's philosophy, which allows us to develop a highly individualized curriculum and cultivate the right teaching environment for your child.

With the guidance of highly trained proffessional teachers, we offer a wide array of courses from language arts to mathematics to botany to computer skills. Our teachers work very hard to make sure each and every child receives individual attention to progress every day.

It's our promise at Honeycomb Montessori.

LANGUAGE

Our language program provides an essential foundation for reading, writing, and the functions of the English language. Your child will be introduced to sounds and letters on a written, visual, and vocal level. Your child will also learn to blend these sounds and letters to pronounce new words and to read at a phonetic level. All of our language exercises will use the following interactive montessori equipment:

GEOGRAPHY

Our geography program offers very practical knowledge to all beginner students. The course introduces the student to the geographic world and all of its structures. The child learns to indentify land forms, bodies of water, and conceptual terms used in everyday life like directions, continents and dates. By the end of the course, your child will have a greater understanding of their surroundings and the world. All geographic concepts will use the following interactive montessori equipment:

  • PREPARATION
  • LEFT HAND/RIGHT HAND
  • DIRECTIONS (N,E, S, W)
  • CALENDAR OVERVIEW
  • PRESENTATION OF CLOCK/CLOCK EXERCISES
  • WEATHER AND TEMPERATURE
  • FUNDAMENTAL NEEDS OF A MAN
  • LAND AND WATER FORMS
  • GLOBES
  • PUZZLE MAPS
  • TIME LINES
  • DIRECTION CARDS
  • AREA MAP
  • FLAG EXERCISES

BOTANY & ZOOLOGY

Botany and Zoology is a unique program that teaches students basic biology and animal habitats. The student will learn to recognize different trees, animals, and mammals. Your child will also learn to categorize these animals into groups. Botany and Zoology will use the following interactive montessori equipment:

  • SCIENCE
  • MAGNET WORK
  • SINK OF FLOAT
  • MAGNIFYING GLASS WORK
  • WEIGHTS AND MEASURES
